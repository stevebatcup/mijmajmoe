Mij-Maj-Moe is a simple game using Emojis as characters in a game of Tic-Tac-Toe.
Once you've downloaded the app here's how to play...

  1. Click NEW Game
  2. Choose Emoji for player 1 by selecting the icon for player 1 and selecting from the popup list.
  3. Do the same for player 2
  4. Press START
  5. Enjoy your game
